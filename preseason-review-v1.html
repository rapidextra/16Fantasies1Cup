3whits – 2025 Preseason Roast

A deep dive into a draft strategy best described as "boldly mediocre."

Manager Profile

Manager: 3whits

2024 Finish: 11th Place (Consolation Bracket Runner-Up)

Draft Grade: C-

Draft Strategy

3whits went for a classic "Stars and Scrubs" approach, securing two certified studs in CeeDee Lamb and Josh Allen in the opening rounds. This gives his team a high ceiling every week. However, the strategy fell apart shortly after, as he chased upside with a series of questionable picks and risky dart throws. The lack of reliable depth at RB and TE could be a significant weakness, leaving him vulnerable to injuries and bye weeks.

“His first two picks were filet mignon. The rest of the draft was gas station sushi.”

Bolters & Spuds

BOLTERS

  • CeeDee Lamb (WR): An absolute fantasy monster. Elite.
  • Josh Allen (QB): The overall QB1 is a league-winner.
  • James Cook (RB): Could thrive as the lead back in Buffalo.
  • George Pickens (WR): Has the talent to be a top-15 WR if he gets the targets.

SPUDS

  • Tank Bigsby (RB): Already looks cooked.
  • Brenton Strange (TE): Irrelevant dart throw.
  • Detroit Lions (DEF): Drafting a defense this early is spud behaviour.
  • Marvin Mims (WR): All sizzle, no steak.

Final Word

3whits has lived both sides of the fantasy coin — contender in ’23, flop in ’24. His 2025 draft starts strong with Lamb and Allen, but fades quickly into dart throws and spuds. A couple of Bolters could drag him into the playoffs, but history says he’s more likely to fade into the background again. A safe, middle-of-the-pack finish seems almost inevitable.

This has been a preseason roast. Please don't cry.
